## Claim: The Catholic Church added 7 books to the Bible

~ False.  The Biblical canon was set to 73 books at the Synod of Rome in A.D. 382; the Council of Hippo in A.D. 393; the Council of Carthage in A.D. 397; a letter from Pope Innocent I to Exsuperius, Bishop of Toulouse in A.D. 405; the Second Council of Carthage in A.D. 419; the Second Council of Nicaea in A.D. 787; and the Second Council of Florence in A.D. 1438 to A.D. 1445.
